Responsibilities
==============================================
A student has the following primary responsiblities inside checksheet management system:

 * Ensure that their checksheet is updated every semester especially before they make an advising appointment.
 * Add notes inside their checksheet denoting the semester they took the class and any other information that they might want for themself or their advisor to know.
 * Request advising session at least a week ahead once their advisor notifies them that advising session has begun.

 These tasks can be handled from the intiuitive student user dashboard which will be discussed in the next section.
